Lazy Vs Eager

- Lazy Loading: Data is loaded on demand when it is accessed.
  - Only required queries are called.
  - Lazy Loading delays the loading of related data until it is actually needed or accessed in the code.
- Eagger Loading: Data is loading immediately when the entity is fetched.
  - Extra Queries are called.

### Cascade

In JPA, cascade refers to the operations that are automatically propagate from a parent entity to its related to child entities.
